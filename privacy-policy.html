Privacy Policy

RoomQuest - Secure, Transparent, Reliable

RoomQuest is a mobile application developed for Hotel Reservation . The app helps users discover and book hotel rooms conveniently.

1. Information Collection and Use

RoomQuest uses Firebase services, including Authentication and Firestore. We collect the following information:

  • Email address (used for login and authentication)
  • Room booking preferences

This information is used strictly for app functionality and is not shared with any third parties.

2. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely using Firebase's infrastructure. We do not store any sensitive data locally on the device.

While we use trusted systems to protect your information, no system is 100% secure. We take reasonable precautions to keep it safe.

Our app may use other Firebase services such as Firestore and Firebase Analytics. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.
